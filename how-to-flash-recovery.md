# 🔧 How To Flash From Custom Recovery

## Steps

1. **Prepare your files**  
   Place the ZIP on your SD card, internal storage, or OTG device.

2. **Format data / Factory reset**

3. **Flash the ZIP**  
   Select and flash it from your custom recovery.

4. **Wait for the process to complete**  
   Flashing may take a while — any errors during this step are generally fine.

5. **Reboot to recovery**  
  it will changed to oss recovery.

6. **Reboot system**

---

## 📝 Notes

- **OrangeFox users:** Do **not** tick *"Reflash OrangeFox"* after flashing your ROM.
- **Flashing from PC or Bugjaeger?** You can use **[`adb sideload`](https://github.com/mt6833-dev-transsion/release/blob/main/how-to-flash-sideload.md)** instead.
- **Firmware requirement:** Make sure you're on **HiOS 14** firmware before flashing.
- **Recommended:** I recommend to use **OSS Recovery/Aosp recovery**.

# 🔧 How To Flash From OSS Recovery/Aosp Recovery

## Steps

1. **Prepare your files**  
   Place the ZIP on your SD card or Flashdisk or something like that.

2. **Format data**

3. **Choose "Apply Update"**

4. **Select your source**  
   Choose *"from SD card"* or *"from OTG"*.

5. **Flash the ZIP**

6. **Wait for the process to complete**  
   Flashing may take a while — any errors during this step are generally fine.

7. **Reboot system**

---

## 📝 Notes
- **Flashing from PC or Bugjaeger?** You can use **[`adb sideload`](https://github.com/mt6833-dev-transsion/release/blob/main/how-to-flash-sideload.md)** instead.
- **Firmware requirement:** Make sure you're on **HiOS 14** firmware before flashing.
